About HourGlass

We built HourGlass to help you get your time back.

In Spring 2020, when the world was hit with a global pandemic, we noticed that people were working remotely like never before. We realized employees were having a hard time balancing home and work life, spending what felt like endless hours online.

We set out to create a tool to help counter this, putting users back in control of their time. HourGlass gives people the ability to help see extactly how much time they spend online for the first time. Our mission is to answer the question of wondering where your day has gone.

Check it out and take your time back into your hands!
